# Treacon Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 13, 2026_

Treacon does not collect, store, or transmit any data. Period.

- **No data collection.** The extension has no analytics, no tracking, no telemetry, and no error reporting.
- **No servers.** Everything happens locally in your browser. The extension makes zero network requests.
- **Minimal scope.** It only runs on `threads.com` and `threads.net`, and requests no other browser permissions.
- **Local processing only.** Thread text is read from the page you are viewing, reformatted in-page, and shown in an overlay. Nothing leaves your browser.
- **Clipboard.** Text is written to your clipboard only when you click a copy button. The extension never reads your clipboard.

Since no data is collected, there is nothing to share, sell, or delete.
